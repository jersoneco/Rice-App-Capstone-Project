        Magnaporthe grisea, also known as rice blast fungus, rice rotten neck, rice seedling blight, blast of rice, oval leaf spot of graminea, pitting disease, ryegrass blast, Johnson spot, neck blast, and Imochi is a plant-pathogenic fungus and model organism that causes a serious disease affecting rice.

CAUSE
        Fungus Pyricularia Oryzae.

SYMPTOMS
        White to gray-green lesions or spots with brown borders.

SOLUTION
        Triazole and Strobilurin fungicides.
