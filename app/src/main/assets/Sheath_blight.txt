        Infected leaves senesce or dry out and die more rapidly, young tillers can also be destroyed. the leaf area of the canopy can significantly be reduced by the disease. This reduction in leaf area, along with the diseased-induced senescence of leaves and young infected tillers are the primary causes of yield reduction.

CAUSE
        Basidiomycetous necrotroph Rhizoctonia solani.

SYMPTOMS
        Elliptical spots with light-colored centers and reddish edges.

SOLUTION
        Validamycin and Polyoxin fungicides.
