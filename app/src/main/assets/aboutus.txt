
    The Rice Pro app is developed to help in the rice agriculture industry, primarily for the benefit of farmers. We, the creators, are dedicated to provide solutions to common problems in rice farming, such as prevalent diseases in rice plantations, inappropriate fertilizer application rates, and the difficulty to sustain good rice crop production. This app easily identifies the disease that damages the rice crops, offers a reliable rice cultivation guide that assists farmers in efficiently managing their rice farms, and provide essential information to promptly avert the propagation of the disease that ruins the quality of the rice crops. Furthermore, this app effortlessly generates an accurate calculation of fertilizer application rate, which is required for adequate nutrient uptake of the rice plants.
